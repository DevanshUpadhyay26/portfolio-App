About Me

I'm a passionate Computer Science and Business Systems student at Bharati Vidyapeeth Deemed To be University, with a strong focus on full-stack development and machine learning. My experience spans from developing Django-based web applications to working with cutting-edge AI technologies. As a recent SDE Intern at SRFusion Tech, I honed my skills in Django REST framework and API testing. Previously, I led the development of a lab inventory system at Red Fibre, improving reservation processing by 30%.

My technical toolkit includes Python, C++, JavaScript, Django, Flask, and various database technologies. I'm particularly proud of my work on 'Heart Care', an early heart attack prediction system using gen AI, which secured 50,000 Rupees in seed funding. I'm also a budding researcher, having co-authored a paper on 'Early Heart Attack Prediction Using Transformers' and filed a patent application for a heart attack risk prediction system. Always eager to learn and innovate, I'm seeking opportunities to apply my skills in challenging tech environments and contribute to groundbreaking projects.
